Acknowledgement of Traditional Owners and Country

The Atlas of Living Australia acknowledges Australias Traditional Owners and pays respect to the past and present Elders of the nations Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander communities. We honour and celebrate the spiritual, cultural and customary connections of Traditional Owners to country and the biodiversity that forms part of that country.
